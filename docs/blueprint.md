# **App Name**: J&G Exports Landing

## Core Features:

- Homepage Video: Display an engaging video showcasing coffee bean selection to capture visitor's attention.
- About Us Section: Present company background and mission with integrated design and motion.
- Services Showcase: Describe services provided (e.g., export, wholesale, distribution).
- Core Values: Detail unique selling points: why choose J&G Exports (quality, reliability, etc.)
- Product Gallery: Display J&G's coffee product portfolio with a rotating carousel and large imagery.
- Coffee Grading: Classification by categories: bean type, roast, or certification.
- Export Process: Detail the export process steps.
- QA and Capacities & Logistics: Showcase the company's quality assurance, capacities, and logistics capabilities.
- Contact Form: Provide contact information and a submission form for inquiries.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary background color: Black (#000000).
- Secondary font color: White (#FFFFFF).
- Tertiary color: bc994f.
- Headline font: 'Big Shoulders Display' (sans-serif) for bold and impactful titles. Note: currently only Google Fonts are supported.
- Body font: 'Montserrat' (sans-serif) for a clean and readable text. Note: currently only Google Fonts are supported.
- Use parallax scrolling effects to reveal the beauty of J&G exports.
- Add page transition animations to smoothly go to the required section, such as a smooth transition between sections upon scrolling.
- Gold accent color (#C8A24A)
- Headings: tall condensed sans-serif (similar to Bebas Neue or Anton)
- Body text: modern geometric sans-serif (similar to Inter or Montserrat)
- Match the reference images in composition, positioning, margins, and proportions
- Use image grids on the right and text blocks on the left
- Keep horizontal separator lines
